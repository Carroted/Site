About Us

Carroted is a group of people who make games, software, animation, products, and more.
We are passionate about making open-source software, and we are always looking for new ways to make our work better.

We are currently working on several projects, so keep an eye out!


Carroted was started in 2021, and we released our first game Pylon Pixel on December of the same year.
