Carroted Games


This is a list of games released by Carroted.

 Pylon Pixel
by Alex_Sour and Lynix

Featuring over 50 levels, a story, achievements to unlock, an Endless Mode and more, Pylon Pixel is a really great experience on Android!

It was developed by Alex_Sour, with help from Lynix (not the YouTuber, but a Discord user of the same name) and released on the 30th of December, 2021.

 Pylon Pixel Lite
by Alex_Sour

Pylon Pixel Lite was a game originally designed to be a smartwatch-only companion app for Pylon Pixel, but was instead used as a demo of Pylon Pixel that also included phone/tablet support.

It is an Android exclusive.

Money Hover Cube Bank
by Alex_Sour

Money Hover Cube Bank is a game about a person who has been given a loan of 100$ and has to hover over green shapes to gain more money to not go into debt.

It was made on the 18th of March, 2022.
