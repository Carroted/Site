FROM SUNSTEEL

From Sunsteel is a text RPG game, set on the fictional world of Duscolia.



From Sunsteel is a console text RPG built using C#. It is free, open source and under the MIT license.

About

Set on the fictional world of Duscolia in a time where thousands of tribes are at war over the remaining supplies of Sunsteel, an incredibly strong and versatile metal. Your tribe recently left for the Sunsteel Hills, a site holding the remaining supplies of Sunsteel. You decided to desert your tribe, however, and seek adventure across Duscolia instead.
